FAQ

Common Questions.

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Does AI really apply to my business? +
Almost certainly yes. 82% of very small businesses that think AI doesn't apply to them are wrong — it's an education gap, not a real limitation. The assessment is specifically designed to uncover where the opportunities are in your specific situation.
We already use ChatGPT. Do we still need this? +
That's a great starting point — but using ChatGPT ad hoc is very different from having AI embedded in your actual workflows. Most businesses are leaving 80% of the value on the table. We find it and build it.
How much work is required on our end? +
Minimal. You show up for the discovery call and a follow-up review — we handle the research, analysis, and implementation. That's by design. Your time is exactly what we're trying to protect.
What if we're not ready to implement after the assessment? +
No problem at all. The $1,000 assessment gives you a complete, actionable roadmap regardless of what you decide to do next. Many clients implement on their own using the plan we deliver. There's no obligation to continue.
Can we skip straight to the retainer (Tier 3)? +
Absolutely. Larger or more sophisticated organizations can jump straight into an ongoing advisory relationship. We'll do a discovery call to understand where you are and build the retainer scope around your specific needs.
What size businesses do you work with? +
Primarily small businesses — typically working directly with the CEO or CTO. We're built for lean teams where AI can have outsized impact without massive IT infrastructure or budgets.
What does the assessment actually cost all-in? +
The assessment is a flat $1,000. There are no hidden fees, hourly overages, or surprise costs. What we find typically saves multiples of that in the first month of implementation alone.
How long does implementation take? +
The Implementation Sprint (Tier 2) typically runs 2–4 weeks depending on complexity. We scope it based on the top 2–3 findings from your assessment so you know exactly what you're getting before we start.
